Instructions


English

  • One person alone or as a group of a maximum of four people can submit ideas.
  • The team is not mandatory.
  • You can submit as many ideas as you want.
  • Faculty and non-teaching staff can also participate along with the students.
  • It is compulsory to mention your name, department, semester, and phone number.
  • If the idea is being submitted as a group, give a name for your group and also mention your teammate's name, branch, or department.
  • The name of the group and details of the group leader are compulsory.
  • Ideas should be submitted by putting them in the idea box or by clicking "Submit Idea" button given below
  • Idea should be submitted before October 20, 2023 (20/10/2023).
